Introducing Precious HMO Mobile

Precious HMO Mobile was launched in 2022 to empower the enrollee with access to self service. The enrollee can complete enrollment in 5 mins, access to digital ID card, track medical history, view benefit package, change hospital, add dependents, and much more. The app is currently available on Google play store for Android users.
